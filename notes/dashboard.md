## Physics
- Three processes
	- Bremsstrahlung
		- Effectively splits
	- Pair Production
		- Catastrophic loss
			- All energy goes into the electron-positron pair
		- Only occurs for photon energies $>1 \pu{MeV}$
	- Ionization
		- only process where energy is lost to the medium
